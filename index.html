Background

Kirirom Institute of Technology (KIT) is a private institution for higher education located in Kirirom National Park, Cambodia founded by Mr. Takeshi Izuka. The institute was established in 2014 and formally commenced operations in October 2014. The Institute is part of A2A Town Co, Ltd. that envisions the development of Southeast Asiaâ€™s counterpart to Silicon Valley. KIT plans to develop students who can use cutting edge technologies to innovate the world. It further aims to provide opportunities to hard working and talented students who may be in the bottom of the pyramid instead of serving the privileged few.
